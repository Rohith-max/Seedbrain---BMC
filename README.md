# NIDHI

## The Operating System for Indian Families

NIDHI is a family intelligence platform built specifically for Indian households.

It organizes documents, tracks obligations, identifies opportunities, and helps families manage important decisions across finance, healthcare, education, property, insurance, and government services.

Unlike traditional document storage systems, NIDHI actively understands household information and converts it into actionable intelligence.

---

## The Problem

Indian households manage a large number of critical documents and obligations across multiple family members.

Common challenges include:

* Documents scattered across phones, WhatsApp, email, physical files, and cloud storage
* Missed insurance renewals and premium payments
* Forgotten tax deadlines
* Lost scholarship opportunities
* Unclaimed government benefits
* Difficulty locating documents during emergencies
* Lack of centralized family knowledge
* Language barriers when accessing services

The result is financial loss, missed opportunities, and unnecessary administrative burden.

---

## The NIDHI Approach

NIDHI acts as a Family Operating System.

The platform:

* Organizes family documents
* Extracts structured information
* Tracks deadlines and obligations
* Detects scheme eligibility
* Identifies missing documents
* Provides proactive recommendations
* Supports multilingual interactions
* Creates a centralized household intelligence layer

---

## Core Capabilities

### Document Intelligence

Native support for Indian household documents:

* Aadhaar
* PAN
* Passport
* Driving License
* Voter ID
* LIC Policies
* Bank Passbooks
* Property Records
* Tax Documents
* Medical Records
* Educational Certificates

NIDHI automatically extracts metadata, identifies expiry dates, and classifies documents.

---

### Family Intelligence

NIDHI understands relationships across:

* Parents
* Children
* Grandparents
* Dependents
* Nominees
* Joint family structures

The system maintains a living household knowledge graph connecting people, documents, assets, obligations, and opportunities.

---

### Government Scheme Intelligence

Examples include:

* PMAY
* Ayushman Bharat
* PM Kisan
* Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana
* National Scholarships
* Senior Citizen Benefits

The system highlights opportunities and identifies missing requirements.

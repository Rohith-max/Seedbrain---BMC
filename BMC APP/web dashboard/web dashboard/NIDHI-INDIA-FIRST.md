## Government Scheme Intelligence Engine

### AI-Powered Eligibility Detection

The system automatically analyzes family profile and documents to identify eligible government schemes:

#### Major Schemes Supported

1. **Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY)**
   - Housing subsidy up to ₹2.67 lakh
   - For economically weaker sections
   - Requires: Income Certificate, Property Documents

2. **Ayushman Bharat - PMJAY**
   - ₹5 lakh annual health insurance
   - For low-income and uninsured families
   - Requires: Aadhaar, Ration Card

3. **Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana**
   - 7.6% returns, tax-free savings for girl child
   - Account opens until age 10
   - ₹21+ lakh maturity at age 21

4. **PM Kisan Samman Nidhi**
   - ₹6,000 annual income support for farmers
   - 3 installments of ₹2,000 each
   - Requires: Land Ownership Proof

5. **National Scholarship Portal Schemes**
   - Up to ₹2.5 lakh annually for students
   - Merit and need-based scholarships
   - Requires: School/College ID, Income Certificate

6. **Senior Citizen Welfare Schemes**
   - Pension, healthcare, travel concessions
   - Eligibility: Age 60+
   - State and national-level benefits

7. **Disability Welfare Schemes**
   - Pension, vocational training, aids & appliances
   - Requires: Disability Certificate

### Eligibility Scoring

Each scheme gets a scoring system (0-100%):
- **90-100%**: Eligible, all documents present
- **70-89%**: Near eligible, missing 1-2 documents
- **50-69%**: Potentially eligible, significant documentation needed
- **Below 50%**: Not currently qualified

### Proactive Alerts

The system surfaces:
- "You may qualify for this scheme"
- "You have 80% of documents required"
- "You're missing: Income Certificate"
- "Deadline approaching: Apply by June 30"

- **SIP Investments**: Systematic Investment Plan tracking
- **FD Maturity**: Fixed deposit maturity dates
- **Credit Card Due**: Monthly due dates and payment tracking
- **Utility Bills**: Electricity, water, internet bills
- **Insurance Renewal**: Auto, home, health insurance
- **School Fees**: Educational institution fees

### Financial Insights
